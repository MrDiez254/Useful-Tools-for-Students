## Notion
Team Member 2 - Isaac Macharia.
- Most note-taking apps feel like a blank sheet of paper—you type from top to bottom, and that is about all you can do.

- Notion is completely different. Notion is like a digital bucket of Lego blocks.

- Instead of just writing text, everything you create in Notion is an individual block that you can choose, customize, and snap into place.

### The 4 Basic "Lego Bricks" of Notion
- When you open a page in Notion, you decide what type of block to drop onto the screen:

- Text Blocks: Standard paragraphs for typing out your regular notes.

- Media Blocks: Pieces that allow you to drag in images, code snippets, or video links directly onto the screen.

- Database Blocks: High-level blocks like spreadsheets, calendars, or boards where you can track assignments and deadlines.

- Page Blocks: The ultimate Lego block. In Notion, you can put a page inside of a page. This means you can create one master page for your Academy, and drop separate "sub-page" blocks inside it for different classes. 
**Notion is an all-in-one workspace app where you can take notes, track projects, and organize your life.**
*Notion’s entire text editor runs on Markdown.*
